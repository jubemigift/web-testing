What is Global Pathway Visa?

GLOBAL PATHWAY VISA SERVICES is a professional recruitment consulting company based in Nigeria. Our primary goal is to bridge the gap between skilled Nigerian individuals and employment opportunities, both locally and internationally. We act as your dedicated partner, guiding you through the entire process from job searching and application to securing your visa and preparing for your new role. We are committed to providing a transparent and supportive pathway to your career success.

How long does the Job application process take?

The length of the job application process can vary significantly depending on several factors, including the country of employment, the specific industry, and the employer's internal hiring timeline.

What documents do I need for my visa application?

The required documents for a Job application are specific to the description of the job and the type of job you are applying for (e.g., a skilled worker , a temporary work, etc.). However, here is a general list of documents that are almost always required:

  • A valid Indentity with a minimum of six months of validity.
  • A completed job application form/letter (which we will assist you with).
  • Recent passport-sized photographs that meet the country's specific requirements.
  • Proof of qualifications and work experience relevant to the job.
  • Medical examination report (if required by the destination country).
  • Educational work experience, if needed
