About FoodPick

Our Story

Founded in 2024, FoodPick was born from a passion for authentic Nigerian cuisine and the desire to bring fresh, delicious meals to the people of Warri and environs.

We believe that great food should be accessible to everyone, delivered quickly, and prepared with the highest standards of hygiene and quality.

From traditional jollof rice and native soups to modern grills and wraps, we celebrate the rich culinary heritage of Nigeria while embracing contemporary tastes.
